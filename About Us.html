About Misha Foodstuffs

Your Trusted Korean Food Mart and Distributor in Nairobi – Misha Foodstuffs Ltd

Welcome to Misha Foodstuffs Ltd, Nairobi’s leading supplier of authentic Korean food products. Located on Samaki Drive Number 13, we are proud to be Kenya’s trusted Korean food mart and wholesale distributor, bringing the bold, rich flavors of Korea to homes, restaurants, hotels, cafes, schools, and institutions across the country.

We offer a curated selection of premium Korean groceries, including:

  • Traditional Kimchi
  • Instant Korean Ramen
  • Korean Sauces and Pastes
  • Rice Cakes (Tteok)
  • Frozen Meats for Korean BBQ
  • Korean Snacks and Beverages

All our products are imported from reputable suppliers and meet strict quality standards, ensuring the authentic taste of Korea in every bite. we’re here to support you with fast delivery, competitive prices, and great customer service.

At Misha Foodstuffs, we proudly serve a diverse clientele — from supermarkets, butcheries, and cafes to international schools and fine dining restaurants. Whether you’re restocking your store or crafting a Korean-inspired menu, we offer:

  • Fast delivery across Nairobi and Kenya
  • Competitive wholesale prices
  • Friendly and reliable customer service

Discover why chefs, retailers, and food lovers choose Misha Foodstuffs Ltd – Kenya’s home for Korean ingredients.
